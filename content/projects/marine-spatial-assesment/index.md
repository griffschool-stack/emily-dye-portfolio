## Project Overview
During my **Marine Spatial Assessment Internship** at the **Ecological Oceanography Lab**, I developed environmental spatial analyses for proposed deep-sea mining sites.

* **Bathymetric Modeling:** Generated a map of the **Cook Islands Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ)** bathymetry.
* **Habitat Visualization:** Mapped sea surface temperature and chlorophyll concentration using the **NOAA ERDDAP** database.

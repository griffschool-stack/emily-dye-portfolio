## Project Overview
Serving as a **Community Fisheries Biological Science Assistant** for the **Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife**, I conducted critical monitoring of native fish populations.

## Key Responsibilities
* **Biological Surveys:** Conducted wildlife surveys using **electroshock fishing**, snorkel observations, and winter **Steelhead redd monitoring**.
* **Salmonid Research:** Organized tagging of **Chinook salmon** at South Santiam fish hatchery.

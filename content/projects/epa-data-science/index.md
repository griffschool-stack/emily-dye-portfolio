## Project Overview
As an **Undergraduate Volunteer** for the **U.S. Environmental Protection Agency**, I specialized in large-scale aquatic data processing and geospatial modeling.

## Technical Contributions
* **Data Cleaning:** Analyzed 4,077 taxonomic entries within the **National Coastal Condition Assessment (NCCA)**.
* **Integrity:** Identified and eliminated redundancies using **R Statistical Language (RStudio)** and **Excel**.
* **Geospatial Modeling:** Used **ArcGIS Pro** and **MATLAB** to delineate elevation gradients of rivers across the conterminous U.S..
